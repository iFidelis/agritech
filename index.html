About the Program

The FCMB Agritech EPIC Pitch seeks to grow the Agricultural sector by providing early to growth-stage entrepreneurs with prerequisite resources to grow and scale their Agritech businesses.

The program will support Agritech businesses in Nigeria, and the rest of Africa, with seed investment, access to workspace, market & network that stimulates innovation, collaboration and business growth.

Due to the coronavirus pandemic, the event would take place online.

Who should apply?

The program is open to Agritech startups in Africa using technology to solve any of the following problems in the Agricultural sector

Processing/Production/Storage

Agricultural Finance

Agricultural Marketing

Supply Chain

What you stand to gain

₦1.5 million grant shared among top 2 startups

Mentor support

Access to workspace, market and networks that stimulate innovation, collaboration and business growth.

Access to Passion Incubator’s resources, partner organization support and business development services
